Stardew Valley 1.6 Update

Stardew Valley creator Concerned Ape has been teasing the Stardew Valley 1.6 content update since it was announced in April 2023 ranging from one-word clues to a whole content list. Though new content is an extremely exciting prospect for this update, Concerned Ape also mentioned that this update will be "mostly changes for modders (which will make it easier & more powerful to mod)." See what we know below!

New Content's

  • A new major festival
  • Two new mini festivals
  • New late-game content, which expands on each of the skill areas.
  • New items and crafting recipes
  • Joja alternatives to some of the end-game quests.
  • 100+ new lines of dialogue
  • Winter outfits for the villagers
  • New type of reward for completing billboard requests
  • (PC) Support for 8 player multiplayer
  • Many small additions and adjustments
  • New farm type
  • New secrets & more

Release Date

Sorry, there's no release date yet!
