About Us

What is Calico Games?


Calico Games is a platform to purchase video games from AAA titles to indie titles. Get exclusive discounts, easy to update games, easy access to your owned games, and many more great Benefits.

Our Story


Once upon a time, there was a kid who wants to buy a game without a hassle. Unfortunately, he can't because of how most of the platform to buy games are complicated to use. In 2023, he then make his own video game platform that are easy to use with affordable price for every games that are sold here. That way he can help others who wants to buy a game that are cheap and the platform is easy to use.
